Unrotten Tomatoes : Top 5 Animation Movies of All Time

~ Reviewed by Chris Chen

  • Spirited Away (2001) ★★★★☆

    • Directed by Hayao Miyazaki, this film is a masterpiece of animation that tells the story of a young girl who becomes trapped in a mysterious spirit world. Its stunning visuals and deep emotional themes have made it a classic.

    Director: Hayao Miyazaki


  • Toy Story (1995) ★★★★☆

    • As the first entirely computer-animated feature film, Toy Story revolutionized the animation industry. Directed by John Lasseter, it tells the story of toys that come to life when humans aren't around, exploring themes of friendship and loyalty.

    Director: John Lasseter


  • Ne Zha (2019) ★★★★★

    • This Chinese animated film that has gained international acclaim for its stunning animation and engaging story. It reinterprets Chinese mythology with a modern twist, focusing on a young spirited kid coming of age.

    Director: Jiao Zi


  • Inside Out (2015) ★★★★★

    • Eleven year old Riley leaving behind her life in Minesota while the movie shows she and her five core emotions in her head struggle to cope throughout the journey.

    Director: Pete Docter


  • The Lion King (1994) ★★★☆☆

    • Disney's original animation classic that tells the story of Simba, a young lion who must reclaim his rightful place as king after the death of his father. Its memorable characters and songs have made it a beloved film for generations.

    Director: Roger Allers and Rob Minkoff
